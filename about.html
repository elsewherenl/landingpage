A curated space for discovering and collecting contemporary photography.

We champion emerging artists who challenge perceptions and reward contemplation. We love art that carries mystery, that lingers in memory and invites deeper reflection. We look for distinctive perspectives that reframe how we see ourselves and our world.

Our Focus

Contemporary work set in context and culture. Subtlety over spectacle.

What We Do

We champion artists by helping tell their stories and make collecting accessible. We want to support artists and help collectors find work that brings joy.

About Elsewhere Collective

Tired of corporate life and passionate about photography, I wanted to build a space to discover and champion new artists. Artists struggle to get seen and tell their stories. Art lovers find discovery and buying difficult. Elsewhere exists to bridge that gap. Based in Amsterdam.

Elsewhere because we seek different viewpoints. Collective because we believe in photography's deeper power and in championing artists with perspectives worth seeing.
