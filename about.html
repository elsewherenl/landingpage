Contemporary work set in context and culture. Subtlety over spectacle.

We started Elsewhere Collective to support photographers and help collectors discover new work and perspectives.

Our ambition is to create a curated space to discover and collect new art.

We are drawn to works that bring us closer to ourselves and our world, works with a sense of mystery that stay with you and continue to unfold over time.
